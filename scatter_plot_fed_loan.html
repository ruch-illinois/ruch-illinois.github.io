Federal Loan Amount

This takes a look at the median income of students earned post-college as it relates to the % of students taking a federal loan. This illustrate the finaicial assistance most students generally need to attend these universities. The purpose was to show what % of students generally need finaicial assistance to attend different schools based on different median income for graduates. This plot slightly shows a negative between shools that have students that have high % usages of federal loans, and earned median income levels. It's an interesting, thing to note because there isn't a definitive reason, why this correlation is there, other than that these schools tend to be private, and have more in the liberal arts category, thus are less incline to produce higher earning majors such as those in the STEM categories.

.

Note about Visualization

This visualization has one slider that controls the median income amount. The goal was to show the school's % of students who use federal loans, as the user in this scene slides median income filter. The scatter plot plot points have further details when you hover over to get the detailed information such as the exact median income and % of students using federal loans for that specific school.
