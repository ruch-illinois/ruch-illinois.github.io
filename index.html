The Economics of California Higher Education

A deeper look into the student journey into the California University financial landscape

Median Income After College

This takes a look at the median income of students earned post-college in the California University System. As seen by the many private and public colleges, there is a wide range of colleges, yet as seen in the visual; a higher education can yield a broad range of potential incomes.

.

Note about Visualization

This visualization is a static visualization, and is intended to pique the viewers interest behind the student's financial journey to higher education. The labels are left off, to allow the user to drop any pre-concieved notions about the cost of colleges, and push the user to explore the data in more detail, in subsequent scenes
