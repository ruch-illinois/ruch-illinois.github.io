The Economics of California Higher Education

A closer look into the student journey into the California University financial landscape

Introduction

With escalating costs of attending college, students must navigate the financial waters of attending college, to select the college that best suits their aspirations in addition to what presents the most economic solution for their financial background. This visualisation takes a look at the common financial considerations a student faces when selecting a college, to help identify the trends and insights behind the public and private universities in California.
